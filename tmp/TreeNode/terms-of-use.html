1. 服务概述

1. Service Overview

TreeNode是由适然科技（Shiran Tech）提供的一款Chrome浏览器插件，旨在为用户提供Notion任务数据库的树形视图展示功能。本服务允许用户以树形结构直观地查看和管理Notion中的任务完成情况。

TreeNode is a Chrome browser extension provided by Shiran Tech, designed to offer a tree view display of Notion task databases. This service allows users to intuitively view and manage task completion status in Notion through a tree structure.
